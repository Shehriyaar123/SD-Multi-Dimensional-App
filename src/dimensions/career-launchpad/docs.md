# Career Launchpad Documentation

## Overview
This dimension is dedicated to job applications, interview tracking, and career progression.

## Architecture
- **Path**: `/src/dimensions/career-launchpad/`
- **Components**: Isolated to prevent cross-contamination with other dimensions.
- **State**: Manages job applications, resume versions, and offer letters.

## Limitations & Roles
- **Student**: Can apply to jobs and track interview status.
- **Teacher**: Can review resumes and conduct mock interviews.
- **Personal**: Can browse career paths and build a personal brand.
- **Admin**: Full platform moderation and job posting management.
