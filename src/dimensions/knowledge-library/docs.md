# Digital Knowledge Library Documentation

## Overview
This dimension is dedicated to semantic search, 3D modules, and LLM synthesis of knowledge.

## Architecture
- **Path**: `/src/dimensions/knowledge-library/`
- **Components**: Isolated to prevent cross-contamination with other dimensions.
- **State**: Manages saved resources, contributions, and citations.

## Limitations & Roles
- **Student**: Can search for resources and save them.
- **Teacher**: Can contribute new knowledge modules and verify information.
- **Personal**: Can access public industry reports and trends.
- **Admin**: Full platform moderation and content curation.
